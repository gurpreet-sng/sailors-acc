This month will focus on mastering design tools, learning how to conduct research and interviews, and transforming insights into wireframes, which will then evolve into fully functional prototypes.

Module 1: Design Fundamentals (Week 1-2)

  • Principles of design: Balance, contrast, alignment, and repetition.
  • Introduction to color theory and its emotional impact.
  • Typography basics: Choosing and pairing fonts effectively.
  • Hands-On Activity: Design a simple poster using design principles.

Module 2: Adobe Photoshop Mastery (Week 3-4)

  • Interface walkthrough and essential tools.
  • Photo editing: Retouching, filters, and adjustments.
  • Creating compositions using layers and masks.
  • Hands-On Activity: Design a social media post and retouch a photograph.

Module 3: Adobe Illustrator Mastery (Week 5-6)

  • Vector art basics: Shapes, paths, and pen tool.
  • Creating logos, icons, and illustrations.
  • Typography effects and advanced tools.
  • Hands-On Activity: Design a logo and a custom icon set.

Module 4: Branding and Identity Design (Week 7)

  • What makes a strong brand identity?
  • Creating mood boards and brand guidelines.
  • Hands-On Activity: Develop a brand identity for a fictional business.
