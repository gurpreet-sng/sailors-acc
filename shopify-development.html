Shopify Development Course Syllabus (Offline)

Duration: 8 Weeks (In-Person Classes, 2-3 days/week)

Level: Beginner to Advanced

Class Format: Interactive Workshops, Live Coding, Group Projects

Week 1: Introduction to Shopify and E-Commerce Basics

Day 1
  • Overview of Shopify and the e-commerce industry.
  • Setting up a Shopify store: Domain, hosting, and basic configurations.
  • Shopify admin panel walkthrough: Products, collections, and orders.
  • Hands-On Activity: Setting up your first store.
Day 2
  • Understanding e-commerce fundamentals: Market trends and business models.
  • Choosing the right Shopify plan for different use cases.
  • Introduction to online payments, shipping, and taxes.

Week 2: Shopify Theme Customization

Day 1
  • Introduction to Shopify Themes: Structure and components.
  • Customizing themes using the Theme Editor.
  • Editing homepage layouts and adding promotional banners.
  • Hands-On Activity: Personalize a Shopify theme for your store.
Day 2
  • Managing products, collections, and categories.
  • Adding and customizing navigation menus and pages.
  • Group Exercise: Create a branded homepage design.

Week 3: Liquid Basics

Day 1
  • Introduction to Liquid: Shopify's templating language.
  • Understanding variables, objects, and tags.
  • Modifying basic Liquid templates: Headers, footers, and product pages.
Day 2
  • Using Liquid loops and filters for dynamic content.
  • Hands-On Coding: Customize product and collection pages with Liquid.

Week 4: Advanced Theme Development

Day 1
  • Building custom sections and blocks for flexible designs.
  • Working with JSON templates for dynamic sections.
  • Styling Shopify themes with CSS and JavaScript.
Day 2
  • Hands-On Project: Build a custom product page.
  • Debugging and troubleshooting theme errors.

Week 5: Introduction to Shopify App Development

Day 1
  • Shopify Developer Account setup.
  • Introduction to Shopify APIs and GraphQL.
  • Building private apps for store-specific functionality.
Day 2
  • Hands-On Coding: Create a basic app to integrate with a Shopify store.
  • Understanding app authentication and permissions.

Week 6: Advanced App Development

Day 1
  • Creating public apps and preparing them for Shopify App Store submission.
  • Integrating third-party APIs into your app.
  • Advanced app features: Webhooks and automation.
Day 2
  • Hands-On Project: Build a custom app for enhanced store functionality.
  • Deploying and managing apps.

Week 7: E-Commerce Optimization & Marketing

Day 1
  • SEO strategies for Shopify stores: Keywords, meta tags, and structured data.
  • Speed optimization techniques for better user experience.
Day 2
  • Marketing strategies for Shopify stores: Email campaigns, discounts, and upselling.
  • Hands-On Activity: Set up marketing integrations in a store.

Week 8: Capstone Project

Day 1
  • Capstone Project Kickoff: Build and customize a fully functional Shopify store.
  • Group Work: Implement a custom theme and app solution.
Day 2
  • Final Presentation: Showcase your Shopify store to the class.
  • Peer and instructor feedback for improvement.
